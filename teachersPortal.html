Who we are and what we

Talkpal is a revolutionary app that brings authentic English instruction directly to your child. Talkpal is---as the same suggests---based on talking. The creators of Talkpal know that the best way to learn a language is not from a grammar text book or from dull readings or boring word lists! Frequently Asked Questions

What devices do I need?

  1. A windows system computer with a webcam
  2. A headset with microphone
  3. Stable Internet connection

What is the recruiting process like?

  1. Application
  2. Interview
  3. Computer System Testing
  4. Pre-demo training
  5. Demo lesson
  6. Trial lesson
  7. Passed - Normal lesson

What is the pre-demo training?

It’s for you to get used to the teaching program. You will learn the operations and some teaching techniques. It will be about 30 min long.

What is a demo lesson like?

The demo is a trial lesson in which you teach one or two our staff. They will provide tips and feedback to help you improve your teaching style. We will also decide whether you are ready to teach or need another demo lesson.

What are the trial lessons?

When you start teaching, the first 3 lessons are considered trials. While conducting the first three lessons, you are observed and assessed. You will officially become a Talkpal teacher after passing three trial lessons.

Will I get paid for these training lessons?

You will be paid 60RMB each for 3 trial lessons. You will receive the payment for training regardless of your final hiring status or decision to join our company.

Do I need to do any lesson plans?

Not necessarily, but you will need to prepare for each class by familiarizing yourself with previewing materials.

How long is a lesson?

A lesson is 30 minutes long.

How many students will I be teaching in a class?

You will be teaching 1-3 student(s) in a classroom. Will I be teaching same students every time? We try to match same teachers with same students in the best way we can, but you should also expect to see a few new faces in every class.

How much do I get paid

The payment for completing lessons consists of two parts: A)The Base Salary (60RMB-70RMB per 30-min lesson) B) The Attendance Bonus (weekly), to reward your constant and timely presence in the class. The bonus for each lessons is 10RMB.

What is the class cancellation policy?

In case of emergency, you may cancel the scheduled class by notifying Talkpal Team at least 24 hours in advance. If the kid(s) need to cancel the scheduled class, the Talkpal Team will contact you at least 24 hours in advance.
