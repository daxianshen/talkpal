A: What do you think of that video game we played yesterday?

  你觉得我们昨天玩的那个游戏怎么样？

B: It was fun and I like the story of it.

  很有趣啊，我也喜欢那个游戏的故事。

A: Is there anything else you like to do when you’re free?

  你空余时间还喜欢做些什么吗？

B: I like listening to music and ice-skating.

  我喜欢听音乐和溜冰。

A: I don’t like ice-skating but I like listening to music.

  我不喜欢溜冰但是我喜欢听音乐。

B: We should go to see a concert one day.

  我们应该某天一起去看个演唱会。

A: Sure but we need to save money first.

  当然但是我们需要先存钱。

B: You’re right, let’s save money first.

  你说得对, 那我们先存钱吧。
