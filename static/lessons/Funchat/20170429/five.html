Scene

A: Good morning, Billy. Could you please take out your homework?

  Billy早上好，你能拿出你的作业吗？

B: I’m sorry, teacher.  I don’t have it.

  老师对不起，我没有。

A: Oh, dear. Why not, Billy?

  啊，怎么了Billy?

B: I got a new dog this weekend, and he ate my homework.

  这周末我有了只新的狗狗，它吃了我的作业。

 

A: Oh my goodness. Then please take out your book and turn to chapter 2.

  啊我的天哪。那拿出你的书翻到第二章吧。

B: I’m afraid I don’t have my book either.

  我的书也没有了。

A: You don’t have your book?  Where is it?

  你没带书？它在哪啊。

B: My new dog peed on it.

  我的新狗狗撒尿在书上了。

 

A: Ew. That is disgusting. Then I am afraid that we are going to have a test.

  咦，好恶心啊。我们马上就要进行个小测试了哦。

B: Ummm, teacher? I apologize, but I don’t have a pencil.

  嗯….老师，非常抱歉，但是我没有铅笔。

A: What happened to your pencil??

  你铅笔又怎么了？

B: The dog chewed it up.

  被狗咬了啊。

 

A: Billy, I have had it!  I don’t believe all these lies.

  Billy, 我受不了了! 我不相信这些假话。

B: But really, teacher.  I am telling the truth!

  但是是真的，老师。我说的是实话啊!

A: Enough! Go to the board and write 100 sentences.

  够了够了！上黑板去写下100个句子吧。

B: Yes, teacher. I’m sorry.

  好的老师。对不起。

A: Billy, WHAT HAPPENED TO YOUR SHOES???

Billy，你鞋子又是怎么了啊???

B: The dog…. He buried them.

  那只狗…它把我鞋子埋到土堆里。
