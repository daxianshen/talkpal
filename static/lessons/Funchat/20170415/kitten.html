I Am A Little Kitten

我是一只小猫咪

 

I am a little kitten small and round

我是一只又小又圆的小猫咪

Here is my tummy here is my mouth

这是我的肚子，这是我的嘴巴

When I am very hungry

当我很饿的时候

Hear me shout meow meow meow meow

听我叫喵喵喵喵
