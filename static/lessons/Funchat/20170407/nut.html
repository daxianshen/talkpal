Nutella cookies


Ingredients:
Flour 面粉
Egg 蛋

                  Nutella  能多益（巧克力酱品牌）

 Baking powder 发酵粉


Equipment:
Cup 杯子

Glass 玻璃杯
Spoon 勺子
Food processor/chopper 食物切碎机
Oven 烤箱
Baking tray 烤盘
Baking paper 烘焙纸


Instructions
Turn on the oven (180C)

启动烤箱至180℃
Prepare the baking tray

准备好烤盘
Put all the ingredients in the food processor

把所有原材料放到切碎机里
Click to mix them

启动切碎机搅拌原材料
Take out a piece and make a ball

拿出一小块做成小球状
Put the cookies on the tray

把曲奇饼放到烤盘上

Flatten them with a glass

用玻璃杯将它们压扁
Put the tray in the oven

把烤盘放到烤箱里

Bake for 12min

烘烤12分钟
