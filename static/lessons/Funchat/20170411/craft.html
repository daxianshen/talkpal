Do a 3 little pigs craft
做三只小猪的手工

What is needed 


Two pieces of paper , Coloring pens , Scissors. 
We are going to make the houses for the 3 little pigs!


需要的材料

两张纸，彩色笔，剪刀

我们现在要为三只小猪做房子！


1.First on the first 3 houses. 3 squares that have triangles on top of them. They will look like this! 

第一个房子，有三个正方形，然后顶部有个三角形。看起来像这样。

2.Next we will cut thin pieces of our paper(Parents help !) These thin pieces of paper will be put on your straw house! You can color them yellows or any color you like!

下一步我们要剪一些薄薄的小长条（家长请帮忙）我们会把这些小长条放在木棍房子上。你可以把它们涂成黄色或者其他你喜欢的颜色。

3.Next we will cut thick Long lines out. These lines will go on your stick house! We can color these brown or any color you like! 

下一步我们要剪一些厚的长条。这些将会成为你的砖房。我们可以把它们涂成棕色或者其他你喜欢的颜色!

4.Next we have to cut out a our red squares ! Color them red and put them on your brick house.

然后我们要剪一些红色的正方形。把它们涂成红色然后贴到你的砖房上去吧。 

5.Finally we have our pigs! Cut out 3 circles and color them pink. These 3 circles will serve as our pigs ! You can put one on each house. 

   And there you have your 3 little pigs craft!

最后我们要做出三只小猪!剪出三个圆然后涂成粉色。它们就是我们的三只小猪了哦，你可以把它们放进不同的房子里。
