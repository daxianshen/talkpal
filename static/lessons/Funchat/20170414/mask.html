Superhero Craft !

超级英雄手工!

 

We are going to make our own masks !

我们现在要做一个属于我们自己的面具啦!

We need paper, construction paper or coloring pens , string and scissors .

我们需要纸，彩色卡纸（或者彩色笔），线和剪刀。

On our first piece of paper, we are going to create a shape. This is going to be the mask of our  superhero!

在我们的第一张纸上，我们要做出一个形状。它将会成为我们的超级英雄面具!

Try to cut out the proper shape for the mask. I will make a rectangle for my mask! Then make sure you leave two circles for eye holes.

试着剪出一个合适的形状。我要把我的面具剪成一个长方形! 你们还要留出两个孔做成眼睛哦。

Next, color your mask with any color you like! My mask will be red and green.

下一步，给你的面具上色！我要涂上红色和绿色。

Now we will cut out our mask and the eye holes. You can use any shape you want from triangle circle or squares

现在我们剪下面具，然后剪出眼睛的两个孔. 当然了，你可以剪成任何形状，三角形方形都可以。

Attach the strings to the end of the mask and Tada ! We can be our very own super hero!

把线绑在面具的末端，戴上面具我们就是超级英雄了!
