Acting Scene 8: Lunchtime

Part 1

A: Hey, Mike, are you ready to go to lunch?

B: Oh, boy, you bet I am. I am so hungry.

A: Me, too. I’m starving.

B: So where do you want to eat?

A: How about that new hamburger place?

A: 嗨，Mike，你准备去吃午餐了吗？

B: 噢对呀，我很饿了。

A: 我也是。我快饿死了。

B: 你想去哪里吃呢？

A: 那个新开的汉堡店怎么样？

 

Part 2

A: They have a cheeseburger with 2 patties, four slices of cheese, and 6 slices of bacon.

B: Oh, yeah. They also have those amazing onion rings with ranch dipping sauce.

A: Or we could go to that pizza place over on First street.

B: Oh, I had their meat lover’s pizza last week.  It was mind blowing.

A: I heard about that. Pepperoni, sausage, bacon, and ham all on the same pizza.

A: 他们有双拼的芝士汉堡，里面有四片芝士和六片培根。

B: 噢是的。他们还有超棒的洋葱圈和牧场蘸料。

A: 我们也可以去第一街那边的披萨店。

B: 我上周吃了他们的肉食爱好者披萨。真是棒极了。

A: 我听说了，同一个比萨饼上有意大利辣味香肠，香肠，熏肉和火腿。

 

Part 3

B: There is also a sandwich shop my brother told me about.

B: They have a 12 inch meatball sub that is covered in marinara sauce and mozzarella cheese.

A: My mouth is watering. But how about we go to Big Tony’s Steakhouse?

B: Yes! They have a 22 ounce steak that is out of this world.

A: And they have those awesome baked potatoes with sour cream and cheddar cheese.

B: 我哥哥还跟我说了家三明治的店。

B: 他们有一个12英寸的肉丸子三明治，覆盖着蕃茄酱和芝士。

A: 我要流口水了。那我们去Big Tony的牛排屋怎么样？ 
B: 对!他们有一块22盎司的牛排上市了。

A: 他们还有一些很棒的烤土豆加上酸酱和芝士。

 

Part 4

B: I can’t wait any longer. Let’s go

A: Hey, can you pick up the tab today? I left my wallet at home.

B: Uh, oh……Me too.

A: Ummmm. I think I have some crackers left in my desk. They are chicken flavored.

B: That sounds amazing!

B: 我等不及了，我们走吧!

A: 嘿，你今天能帮我买下单吗？我钱包放家里了。

B: 呃，我好像也是。

A: 嗯…我桌里好像还有些脆饼干。鸡肉味的。

B: 听起来很棒啊!
