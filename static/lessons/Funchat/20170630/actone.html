Part 1

A: Steven, do you know why I called you up here?

B: Yes, Mr. Jefferson. It’s because I didn’t do my homework.

B: I am really sorry. I was just so busy after baseball practice.

A: Now, Steven, I want you remember that your schoolwork comes before baseball practice.

A: But that’s not why I called you up here.

 

A：Steven. 你知道我为什么叫你来这里吗？

B：是的 Jefferson先生。因为我没做家庭作业。

B：很抱歉。我棒球训练后太忙了。

A：Steven，我希望你能明白你的学习任务比你的棒球训练更重要。

A:  但是那个不是我找你来的原因。
