Acting Scene 5: Birthday Plans

Part 1

A: Hey, Jimmy. What are you doing?

B: Oh, hi, Kevin. I’m trying to decide what to do for my birthday.

B: I want it to be really special.

A: Why don’t you go to a baseball game? That would be fun.

 

A：嗨Jimmy，你在干什么呢？

B：嗨Kevin，我在决定我生日要做些什么。

B：我想要它很特别。

A：为什么不去一下棒球赛呢？肯定会很有趣的。

 

Part 2

B: Nah. I don’t really like sports

B: Plus, the last time I went to a baseball game I got hit by a foul ball.

A: Well there is a new roller coaster at the amusement park. That would be a blast.

B: There will probably be a lot of long lines.

 

B：不了不了。我不怎么喜欢运动。

B：加上之前我去看棒球比赛的时候被球撞了。

A：好吧，游乐园有了新的过山车。肯定会很受欢迎。

B：那可能会有很多人排队。

 

Part 3

B: Also, roller coasters tend to make me nauseous.

A: How about if you go to a nice restaurant?

A: There’s a new sushi restaurant that is supposed to be delicious.

B: Ugh. I hate seafood. The last time I ate sushi I got food poisoning.

 

B：坐过山车也让我觉得很想吐。

A：那去一个很棒的餐厅怎么样？

A：那有个新的寿司店肯定很好吃。

B：呃，我讨厌海鲜啊。上次我吃寿司的时候食物中毒了。

 

Part 4

A: Gee, Jimmy, I give up. I don’t know what you can do.

A: When is your birthday, anyway?

B: Oh, it was yesterday. I’m planning for next year’s party.

B: For some reason, it usually takes me awhile.

 

A：啊，Jimmy我要放弃了。我真的不知道你可以做什么。

A: 你生日是多久呢？

B：噢是昨天呀，我现在是在计划明年的生日聚会。

B: 出于某些原因，总是花费很久的时间准备。
