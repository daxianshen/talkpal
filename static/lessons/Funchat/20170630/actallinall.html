Acting Scene 6: The Confession

 

Part 1

A: Steven, do you know why I called you up here?

B: Yes, Mr. Jefferson. It’s because I didn’t do my homework.

B: I am really sorry. I was just so busy after baseball practice.

A: Now, Steven, I want you remember that your schoolwork comes before baseball practice.

A: But that’s not why I called you up here.

 

A：Steven. 你知道我为什么叫你来这里吗？

B：是的 Jefferson先生。因为我没做家庭作业。

B：很抱歉。我棒球训练后太忙了。

A：Steven，我希望你能明白你的学习任务比你的棒球训练更重要。

A:  但是那个不是我找你来的原因。

 

Part 2

B: Oh, then you must have seen me talking to David.

B: I know that I am not supposed to be talking, but I needed to borrow a pencil.

A: Yes, Steven, you should be quiet during class, but that’s not why I wanted to see you either.

B:  Oh, than you must have seen me copying off of Stephanie’s paper.  

 

B：还有你是不是后来看到我跟David说话了。

B：我知道我不应该说话的但是我需要借一支铅笔。

A：是的，Steven你在上课的时候要保持安静。但是那不是我想见你的原因。

B：噢，那你肯定是看到我我抄Stephanie的试卷了。

 

Part 3

B: I’m so sorry, teacher. It’s just that the assignment was so difficult.

A: Steven, I don’t like you copying.

A: You should ask me if you have a question. But that is still not why I wanted to talk to you.

B: Oh, no. Then you must have seen me punch Johnny. Teacher, he called me a poopface!

 

B：老师我很抱歉。那个作业实在是太难了。

A：Steven，我不喜欢你抄作业。

A：你有问题的时候应该问我。但是那也不是我想找你谈话的原因。

B：啊，那肯定是因为你看到我打了Johnny。老师，他叫我大便脸。

 

Part 4

B: Please don’t tell my mother. She will take away my xbox.

A: Oh my God, Steven. Fighting is not allowed in class, no matter what he called you.

A: But the reason I wanted to talk to you is to remind you to bring your permission slip for the field trip tomorrow.

B: Ohhhhh….Okay. I was just kidding about that other stuff. See you tomorrow, Mr. Jefferson.

 

B：希望你别告诉我妈妈。她会拿走我的Xbox的。

A：我的天，Steven在教室里打架也是不允许的啊，不管他叫你什么。

A：但是我找你谈话是想提醒你明天的考察旅行你要记得带许可证哦。

B：噢…好的，我刚刚说的那些都是开玩笑呢。明天见，Jefferson先生。
