How to make cookies

 

Ingredients 配料

Biscuits 饼干

Powered sugar 白糖粉

An orange 一个橘子

Butter 黄油

Cocoa 可可粉

 

Equipment 工具

Large bowl 大碗

Plate 盘子
Spoon 勺子

Knife 刀

Rolling pin 擀面杖


Instructions 操作指南

1. Use a rolling pin to crush the biscuits

用擀面杖碾碎饼干

2. Mix crushed biscuits and powered sugar

将碾碎的饼干和糖混合在一起

3. Squeeze an orange

将橘子挤出汁

4. Add some butter and orange juice

加一点黄油和橘汁

5. Make a ball shape and roll in cocoa

揉成球状在可可粉里滚一滚
