A: Hello, there. Where would you like to go?

嗨你好，你想去哪里啊？

B: Hi. Could you take me to 431 West Second street, please?

嗨，你能带我去西二街431号吗？

A: Yes, sir. Right away. 432 West First street.

可以啊先生。马上去西二街432号。

B: I’m sorry. I think you misheard me.

对不起。我觉得你好像听错了。

    

B: I said 431 West Second street. It’s on the corner of Second and Hill street.

我说的是西二街431号。它是在second and hill街的路口。

A: Oh, I’m sorry about that. Alright, the corner of Second and Kill street.

噢对不起。好的，second and kill街的路口。

B: Kill street?? No, no. Hill street.

Kill街? 不不不，是Hill街。

B: Where, in the world, is Kill street?

世界上哪里有kill街啊？

 

A: I don’t know. You wanted to go there.

我不知道啊。你想去那里。

B: I changed my mind. Just take me to the bank on Main street.

我改变注意了。就带我去主街的银行吧。

A: Gotcha. Take you to see Hank who has a pain in his feet.

知道了。带你去看脚疼的Hank

B: What?! No! Are you doing this on purpose?

什么？？不是! 你这样是故意的吗？

 

A: Doing what? I’m sorry, sir. You will have to speak up. I’m not wearing my hearing aid.

什么？对不起先生。你得说大声一点。我没带我的助听器。

B: I said take me to the BANK on MAIN STREET.

我说带我去主街的银行。

A: Ok, ok. There is no need to yell. The bank on Main street.

好的好的，没必要大嚷嚷。主街的银行。

B: FINALLY.. …..I’m sorry for yelling.

终于对了...对不起我大喊了。

A: So what happened to his feet?

他脚怎么样了啊？
