Scene

A: Hello, Mr. Johnson. Nice to see you again. What can I do for you today?

你好，Johnson先生。很高兴再见到你，有什么我可以帮忙的吗？

B: Well, Doctor, I feel just terrible..

恩，医生，我就是感觉很糟糕。

A: Yes, I can see that. You look very flushed.  

是啊，我看得出来。你的脸看起来很红。

A: You must be running a fever.

你可能发烧了吧。

B: No, Doctor. My temperature is normal. It’s just that I….

不，医生，我的体温是正常的。只是…

A: Well, it must be your ankle. I thought I noticed a limp as you were walking in.

肯定是你脚踝出问题。我觉得你走进来的时候我看到了你有点跛脚。

A: We should put some ice on that.

我们需要用冰敷一下。

B: No, there is nothing wrong with my ankle either. I meant to say….

不，我的脚踝也没有问题。我想说的是…

A: Ahhh. I see it now. It’s your nose. It looks quite big. You must have broken it.

啊，我看到了。是你的鼻子。它看起来很大。你肯定是伤到它了。

B: No. Nothing happened to my nose. It has always looked this way.

不。我的鼻子没问题。它总是这样的。

A: It has? How unfortunate.  

是吗？多不幸啊。

A: I think I hear it now. You have a chest cold. We must take an x-ray right away.

我觉得我知道了。你有支气管炎了。我们现在必须拍个x光。

B: No, Doctor. Really. My body feels just fine.

不，医生，我的身体真的感觉不错。

A: Well, for the love of God, man! Why do you feel so terrible??

好吧，那你到底是为什么感觉不好啊？？

B: I forgot your birthday last week. (Hands him a cake) Happy birthday.

我忘记上周是你的生日了。（递给他一个蛋糕）生日快乐。

A: Oh…Thank you. Now about that nose.

啊，谢谢。关于那个鼻子…
