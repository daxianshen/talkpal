Three Little Pigs



He walked up to the door and banged on the door.

He asked the pigs, “little pigs, Little pigs, let me in.”

The pigs responded , “no! not by the hairs on my chinny chin chin.” 

The wolf got angrier and said “Then I will huff and puff and blow your house in!”

And with a heavy blow he knocked the sticks to the ground!

The two pig brothers ran all the way to the last brothers house. They warned him “ Close the doors! A large wolf is coming !” They cried. But the third brother wasn’t Scared.

“Let the wolf come.” He said to his brothers who were both afraid.

他们警告他，“快关门！一只大灰狼要来啦！”他们哭着说。但是第三个弟弟并不害怕。

“让那只狼来吧。”他对他两个害怕的哥哥说。



The wolf made his way to the last house made of bricks. He was a little tired from blowing down the other 2 houses. He began his chant.

 He asked the pigs, “little pigs, Little pigs, let me in!

The pigs responded , “no! not by the hairs on my chinny chin chin!” 

The wolf got angry and said “Then I will huff and puff and blow your house in!”

The two brothers were afraid but not the last brother.

“You can try” he told the wolf. The brothers closed their eyes. The wolf began to huff and puff but no matter how hard he blew, The house wouldn't fall. He became exhausted, and passed out. The pigs used this chance to tie him up and burned him on his bottom. He ran away and never came back. The 2 brothers moved in with their brother and they lived happily ever after !

他对三只小猪说:“小猪,小猪，让我进来吧”

“你可以试一下。”他跟大灰狼说。两个哥哥吓得闭上了眼睛。大灰狼开始吹，但无论他吹得多用力，这个房子都没有倒下。他累得晕过去了。三只小猪趁机把大灰狼绑起来然后用火烧他的屁股。大灰狼逃跑了，以后都不敢再来了。两个哥哥搬进弟弟的房子里，然后他们开心地生活下去了。
