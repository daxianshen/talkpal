Shopping Online

Joey wants to buy some spicy strips(辣条) online. He turns on the computer and goes to Taobao (淘宝) page.

Wow! So many yummy spicy strips!!! He says.
He adds the goods to his shopping cart. Then he pays it with Alipay(支付宝).

Two days later, the delivery man(快递员) brings him the package.

The spicy strips are so yummy that Joey eats too many.

At night, he starts having a stomach ache.


Oh no! He rushes to the bathroom.
