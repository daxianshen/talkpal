Eric’s travel journal

My friend Tom lived in New York(纽约)5 years ago. His parents worked there. He went to school near Central Park(中央公园).

His house was on Manhattan(曼哈顿) and he had a dog called Fluffy. They invited me to stay with them for the summer holiday.

I visited them in August. I spent two weeks at their place.

We did a lot of interesting things in New York.
