Scene 场景 

A: Excuse me.  Could you help me?

  打扰一下。可以帮帮我吗？

B: Yes, sir. What do you need?

  当然先生，你需要什么？

A: Could you tell me which one is better: the chicken or the pasta salad?

  麻烦你告诉我哪一个比较好，鸡肉呢还是意面沙拉？

B: Well, the chicken tastes better, but the pasta salad is healthier.

  恩，鸡肉更好吃，但是意面沙拉更健康。

A: Then I’ll have the chicken.

  那我点鸡肉吧。

B: Unfortunately, we are all out of the chicken.

  可惜了，鸡肉都卖光了。

A: Well then I guess I’ll have the pasta salad.

   好吧，那我点意面沙拉吧。

B: Ooooh, I’m sorry.  Someone just took the last one.

  啊，对不起。有人刚拿走了最后一份。

A: Oh, my goodness.  What do you have??

  天啊，那你们还有什么？

B: The special today is garlic ice cream!

  今天的特色餐是大蒜冰淇淋!

A: Ugh. No, thank you.

  额，不用了，谢谢。

B: Okay, have a nice day.

好的，祝你今天愉快。
